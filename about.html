About Us

We are storytellers, world-builders, and players first. Every game we make starts with a single question: what would make us forget to sleep?

Our Mission

Make games that
actually matter.

In an industry saturated with sequels and safe bets, Eclipse Interactive exists to prove that small teams can make profound games. We take risks, we experiment, we fail — and we ship anyway, because we believe every imperfect game that tries something new is worth a thousand polished mediocre ones.

We make games for the player who lost an entire weekend to a title they can't stop thinking about — and we make sure our games earn that dedication.

History

Our Journey

2018
The Beginning
Three friends, a shared apartment in Berlin, and a dream. Eclipse Interactive is founded. Neon Blitz, our first game, ships in November.
2019
First Recognition
Neon Blitz wins "Best Debut" at the German Indie Game Awards. The team grows to 5. We begin work on Aethermoor Chronicles.
2021
Aethermoor Launches
Our most ambitious project ships after 2 years of development. 50,000 downloads in the first week. We cry. A lot.
2022
Going Galactic
Void Protocol launches to rave reviews, winning "Best Narrative" at IndieCraft. We hit 200K total players.
2023
Into the Dark
Blackveil ships. Nothing is the same. The horror community adopts us. We are simultaneously delighted and terrified.
2024 →
What's Next
Two new projects in development. One we can't talk about. One we're calling "the biggest thing we've ever done."
